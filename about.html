When Troy showed exteme interest and talent in cutting hair at an early age, his mentors generously allowed him a front-row seat to the barber business

He was hired to sweep floors while taking tips and perfecting his barber skills. He credits them greatly for giving him his start into the business. When he goes to visit them today, they often ask him for a quick cut.

Over the years, Troy has gained loyal clients who he services in a welcoming home enviorment that offers the same "barber shop feels".
